About Us

Welcome to Mumbai Local Mumbai Local is a pioneering initiative by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC), dedicated to empowering Self-Help Groups (SHGs) across Mumbai.

Empowering Communities At the heart of Mumbai Local is the commitment to uplift local communities. By facilitating sales and creating opportunities for SHGs, we enable them to reach wider audiences and achieve financial independence.

Our platform is designed to support and nurture the entrepreneurial spirit of these groups, ensuring that their innovative and high-quality products get the recognition they deserve.

Comprehensive Support Mumbai Local goes beyond just providing a sales platform. We are deeply involved in the marketing and branding efforts to enhance the visibility and appeal of the products.

Our comprehensive support includes: Attractive Packaging: We assist SHGs in designing and creating attractive packaging that stands out in the market.

Promotional Campaigns: Our team develops and executes strategic promotional campaigns to highlight the uniqueness of the products. Media Channels: We utilize various media channels to build a strong brand presence and increase product awareness. Building a Strong Brand

Through our dedicated efforts in marketing and branding, Mumbai Local aims to build a strong and recognizable brand that resonates with consumers. By doing so, we help SHGs establish a solid e-commerce base, ensuring sustained growth and success.
